# Meridian documentation

Meridian connects warehouse feeds, carrier scans, and storefront orders into
one live view of every shipment. These docs cover connecting your first feed,
reading exception data, and automating replies to the questions customers
actually ask.

## How Meridian is organized

- **Feeds** bring data in — warehouse exports, carrier webhooks, storefront
  order streams.
- **Exceptions** are the unit of work: anything that threatens a delivery
  window becomes an exception with an owner and a clock.
- **Views** are saved queries your team shares — "temperature lanes",
  "customs holds this week", "Nordwind's open orders".

## Where to start

If you're evaluating Meridian, [connect a feed](/getting-started) first — the
product only gets interesting with your own data in it. Integrating a
storefront? Jump to the [Exceptions API](/api-reference).

> Data residency, retention windows, and processor agreements are covered in
> the trust pack — ask your account contact for the current PDF.
