# About

I'm Noor — a product designer who prototypes in code. The work I like best
sits where a stressed person meets a dense system: dispatch boards, triage
queues, rostering, quoting. Screens where the honest version of the data is
kinder than the reassuring one.

## How I work

- Research before pixels — the paper list taped to the monitor is usually
  the real spec.
- Prototypes over decks: if a flow can't survive realistic data, it isn't
  designed yet.
- I write the microcopy myself. Error messages are design surface, not
  filler.

## Elsewhere

Talks at IxDA Rotterdam and NUX Manchester on designing for night shifts.
Occasional posts about ops-tool patterns. References on request.
